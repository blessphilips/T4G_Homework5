Pina Colada Smoothie

Made with real coconut milk and pineapple juice. The Pina Colada Smoothie is a refreshing and tropical beverage that combines the flavors of pineapple and coconut, mimicking the classic Pina

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen pineapple
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 Ice cubes (optional)

    • Direction:

  • 1. Prep time: 5 minutes
  • 2. Total time: 5 minutes
